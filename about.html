Pass Information

  1. Single-Day Pass
    • Perfect for fans who can only make it one day
    • Access to all stages and performances on selected day
    • Affordable option for casual attendees and local fans
  2. Full Weekend Pass
    • Access to all performances Friday through Sunday
    • Full festival experience without missing any action
    • Includes general festival merch (commemerative wristband needed for entry)
    • More affordable than buying multiple single-day passes
  3. VIP Weekend Pass
    • Early entry before general admission (beat the crowds!)
    • Exclusive VIP viewing area for all stages
    • Special festival merch pack (t-shirt, poster, limited edition items)
    • Meet & greet opportunities with select bands
    • Full weekend access
